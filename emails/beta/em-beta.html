Hello. You have been chosen to participate in the beta test of the newly created application www.cardition.com. cardition.com is your online cards organizer.
Free Signup
 
 
At cardition.com, you will be able to
 
  • Create folders to store those cards.
  • Sort, filter or search for cards in your folders.
  • Create your own card online and you will receive a unique number (BCN) for this card.
  • Buy a personalised premium BCN.
  • See holders of your own cards.
  • Find the contacts of other users in the global search.
  • Find best products or services next to you and rated and commented by your friends.
 
 
 
A product or service provider could increase his visibility online through cardition.com by
 
  • Creating cards which describe his products or services
  • Personalising his BCN in order to be easily identified
  • Being visible in the global search where people could find him by category, name or other fields
  • Allowing other users to rate, comment and recommend his services or products
  • Having the list of all the holders of his card
 
Join us
 
 
 
As a tester, I would ask you to proceed as follow
 
  • "sign up" to the website
  • fill your profile (only first name, last name, email, and country are mandatory fields)
  • create a card by clicking on "create a business card". If you feel lazy, you can click on "default design" and a card will automatically be created for you otherwise, you can go to "start from scratch" to use your artistic inspiration
  • After your card has been created, you will be given a "BCN". You can also buy a premium "BCN". Don't worry I will give you my credit card number... :-)
  • If you have created a personal or corporate card, please don't change the permission settings as I will need you to be visible in the search for the test
  • If you have created a service and product card, you will have to select the plans in the setting and pay for it. You can use my credit card... all fine!
  • Once you have created your card and receive your BCN, could you please send this BCN to the test mailing list? This will help other testers to load and save your card. Don't worry, all the other testers are trustworthy and respectable people.
  • You can also go in the "Search card(s)" to see the cards created by other testers. For the purpose of this test, I have intentionally left the search opened. By clicking on the magnifying glass icon, you will see all the cards created by the other testers.
